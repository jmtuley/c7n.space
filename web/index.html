c7n.space

Letter-number-letter contractions are … not the best. The collision space for them is huge – for example, is “c7n” short for “confusion?” Or perhaps “chieftan?” (Of course, it’s “collision,” as is “collision space”.)

If you're not sure what contractions to try, consider the common “l10n” and “i18n”. They mean more than just “localization” and “internationalization.”
