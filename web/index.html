c7n.space

Letter-number-letter contractions are dumb. The collision space for them is huge – for example, is “c7n” short for “confusion?” Or perhaps “chieftan?” (Of course, it’s “collision,” but you couldn’t know that.)

This tool exists to highlight the problem. Type in any contraction of this form and you’ll get a list of words that contract to it. Don’t blame me if your favorite word isn’t there; I just pulled the words from /usr/share/dict/words on OSX 10.11.5.

More languages might come in the future.
